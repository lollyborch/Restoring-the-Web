<h2 align="CENTER"><a name="GENERAL"></a>General Information</h2>
<h4 align="CENTER"><b>Gallery Hours:</b></h4>
<center><p>Monday to Sunday 10.00 am - 5.00 pm. Admission free except to
special exhibitions.<br>
Public Holidays: Closed Christmas Day and Good Friday.<br>
Open Anzac Day 12 noon - 5.00pm.</p></center>
<p></p>

<h4 align="CENTER">Information</h4>
<center><p><b>Infoline</b>: 0055 39273 (calls charged at 35c per minute.
Higher rate applies for calls from mobile and public phones). </p></center>
<center><p><b>Information Desk</b> (for general enquiries): 0011 + 61 + 7 + 3840 7303<br>
or  0011 + 61 + 7 + 3840 7350. </p></center>
<h4 align="CENTER"><b>Guided Tours</b></h4>
<center><p>Monday - Friday 11.00 am, 1.00 pm and 2.00 pm<br>
Saturday - Sunday: 11.00 am, 2.00 pm and 3.00 pm <br>
Guided tours for groups of 15 or more may be booked on (07) 3840 7255.<br>
A minimum of 15 days notice is required to secure the services of a guide.<br>
<br>
</p><h4 align="CENTER"><b>Parking</b></h4>
Parking is available underneath the Gallery, with access<br>
from the Stanley Street underpass (off Victoria Bridge).<br>
<br>
<h4 align="CENTER"><b>Wheelchair Access</b></h4>
Wheelchair access is through the elevator on the upper level<br>
of the underground carpark, or via ramps at the front entrance.<br>
Gallery wheelchairs can be booked in advance on (07) 3840 7303.<br><p>
