<p class="text"><font size="4"><strong>C</strong></font>ycling Queensland, in operation since 1893 is the governing body for competetive cycling in Queensland and is affiliated to the national body - Cycling Australia.</p>
<p class="text"><font size="4"><strong>C</strong></font>ycling Queensland has 39 clubs located throughout the state and has 2400 affiliated members.</p>
<p class="text"><font size="4"><strong>C</strong></font>ycling Queensland and its clubs produce annual calenders of events to cover the three disciplines – Road, Track and Mountain Bike race programs. Racing is conducted through club, multi-club and open events, culminating in Queensland State Championships for all three disciplines each year.</p>
<p class="text"><font size="4"><strong>C</strong></font>ycling Queensland caters for members from the Junior Under 11 division through to Masters Men and Women well into their 60’s and 70’s.</p>
<p><span class="text"><font size="4"><strong>C</strong></font>ycling Queensland welcomes new members who may join by becoming a member of one of our Cycling Clubs. For newcomers to the sport, a one-off three ride permit can be purchased, for the prospective member to be able to try their hand at this exciting sport. Once affiliated with Cycling Queensland, the member can compete anywhere in Australia in any Road, Track or Mountain Bike event conducted under the auspices of Cycling Australia. Membership with Cycling Queensland includes personal and public liability insurance cover for racing and training.</span></p>
